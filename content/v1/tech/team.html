Data 8 Technical Team Structure¶

There are many moving pieces in the technology stack used by DSEP, and these are maintained by a team of students and staff. This page describes the roles of the Data 8 tech team and gives a rough idea for the skills that each one should posess.

Infrastructure Staffing¶
